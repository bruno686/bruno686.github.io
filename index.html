Zhuangzhuang He

I am a final-year master's student advised by Prof. Le Wu and Prof. Richang Hong in the Computer Science department at the Hefei University of Technology. Prior to joining HFUT in 2022, I earned my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Anhui Agricultural University.

And many thanks to Prof. Min Zhang and Prof. Peijie Sun for hosting my research visit to the Information Retrieval Group at Tsinghua University during the summer of 2023.

My research interests covers a wide range of topics related to large language models, information retrieval:

  • Large Language Model: Agent, RAG, Multimodal LLM;
  • Data-centric AI: Dealing with noisy data;
  • LLM Applications: LLM for social science, recommendation.
