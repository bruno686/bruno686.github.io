Zhuangzhuang He

I am a second-year master student advised by Prof. Le Wu in the Computer Science department at the Hefei University of Technology. Prior to joining HFUT in 2022, I earned my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Anhui Agricultural University.

And many thanks to Prof. Min Zhang and Dr. Peijie Sun for hosting my research visit to the Information Retrieval Group at Tsinghua University during the summer of 2023.

My research interests covers a wide range of topics related to large language models, OOD Generalization, Recommendation:

  • OOD Generalization: temporal shift;
  • Data-centric AI: dealing with noisy data;
  • LLM Applications: domain-specific LLMs, e.g., recommendation.
I am committed to identifying the truly important problem. Wechat: Stay-Aliving.
